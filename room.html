This spacious studio apartment is an ideal choice for families or groups of up to 4 people. With a room size of 35 sqm, it offers a comfortable and inviting living space. The apartment features a modern design and is equipped with a range of amenities for your convenience and comfort.

The apartment boasts a private balcony with outdoor furniture, providing a pleasant space to relax and enjoy the city views. Inside, you will find a well-equipped kitchenette with a stove, refrigerator, microwave, and cooking utensils. The dining area features a dining table with seating for 4 people.

The room is furnished with 1 double bed, 1 couch bed, and a sofa bed. Also, if it is needed, we can provide a bed for babies or a young child. Bed linen and towels are provided for your stay. Additional amenities include a closet for your belongings, an iron, a hairdryer, and an electric kettle. We also provide coffee and tea.

During your stay, enjoy the convenience of free Wi-Fi and air conditioning. The flat-screen TV with cable channels offers entertainment options for your leisure time. The private bathroom features a shower and is stocked with towels.

At Marianna's Apartments, we strive to ensure a pleasant and comfortable stay for all our guests. Our friendly staff will solve any problem that may occur. Free parking is possible on street outside the building.

Check-in time is from 12:00 PM, and check-out time is until 10:00 AM. Please note that our rooms are non-smoking, and pets are not allowed. Our staff speaks Greek, English, and German, ensuring effective communication during your stay.
