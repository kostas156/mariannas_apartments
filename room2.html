This spacious apartment is perfect for larger groups or families. With a room size of 70 sqm, it offers ample space and comfort for your stay. The apartment features a modern design and is equipped with a range of amenities for your convenience.

The apartment includes a fully equipped kitchen, allowing you to prepare your meals during your stay. The dining area provides a comfortable space for enjoying your meals together. The apartment also features a terrace with view of a playground.

The room is furnished with a queen-size bed, a single bed, a couch bed and a couch, accommodating up to 6 adults. Towels and bed sheets are provided, and there is a closet and some dressers for your belongings. Additional amenities include free Wi-Fi, air conditioning, and a flat-screen TV for your entertainment.

Our friendly staff will help you if any problem occur, ensuring a comfortable stay. On-site self-service laundry facilities are available, and free public parking is provided on the street.

Check-in time is from 12:00 PM, and check-out time is until 10:00 AM. Please note that our rooms are non-smoking, and pets are not allowed. Our staff is fluent in Greek, English, and Russian to assist you during your stay.
